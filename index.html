The Concept of design Art.

Design is designed to create attractive objects for the eye. Although the purpose of each product is different, the majority of people working in design tend to mix practical skills and talents with each other in order to use color, space and shape as best they can.

Design includes a range of specialized areas such as painting, photography, architecture, animation, In general, the artist, designer and craftsman tend to take a career path that suits his passion, develops his individual style, then displays and sells it as a last step
